# Introducing Toggled AI

We're excited to announce the release of our brand new set of artificial intelligence features. These features will enable you to design and develop full websites effortlessly. 

Features
- Design your own prompt
- Choose your site's name
- Create your own color palette or have AI design one for you.

Get Started
To get started navigate to https://toggled.tech/dashboard/ and click `Create`.
