# Billing Info

## Cancelation

If you cancel your subscription, it will be deactivated from that moment and you will no longer have access to premium features. We suggest waiting till the end of your billing period and canceling it there to get the most time out of your plan. Sorry for the inconvenience.

## Refunds

Please contact [info@toggled.tech](mailto:info@toggled.tech) for information on our refund policy and if you qualify for a refund.
